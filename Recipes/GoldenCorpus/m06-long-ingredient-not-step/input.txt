Weeknight Marinara
Ingredients
2 tbsp olive oil
4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
1 28-oz can San Marzano whole peeled tomatoes, drained and crushed by hand
1 tsp dried oregano
fresh basil leaves
Instructions
1. Warm the oil and garlic in a cold pan over medium heat.
2. Add the tomatoes and oregano and simmer 25 minutes.
3. Tear in the basil off the heat.
