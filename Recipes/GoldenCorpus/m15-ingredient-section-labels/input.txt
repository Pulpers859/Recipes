Birthday Vanilla Cupcakes
Ingredients
For the cupcakes:
1 ½ cups cake flour
1 cup sugar
2 large eggs
For the frosting:
2 cups powdered sugar
1 stick unsalted butter, softened
Directions
Cream the butter and sugar until fluffy.
Beat in the eggs one at a time.
Fold in the flour.
Frost the cooled cupcakes.
