Skillet Garlic Butter Shrimp
1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
4 tbsp butter
5 cloves garlic, minced
juice of one lemon
chopped parsley for garnish
Melt the butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant.
Add the shrimp and cook two minutes per side. Squeeze the lemon over the top.
Garnish with parsley and serve immediately.
