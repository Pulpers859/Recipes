Thai Peanut Noodles
Ingredients
8 oz rice noodles
¼ cup creamy peanut butter
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp lime juice
½ tsp red pepper flakes
⅓ cup warm water
Instructions
1. Cook the noodles according to the package.
2. Whisk the peanut butter, soy sauce, lime juice, and water into a sauce.
3. Toss the noodles with the sauce and pepper flakes.
