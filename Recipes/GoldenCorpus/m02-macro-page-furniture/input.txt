GRILLED CHICKEN POWER BOWL
MACROS: 42P / 38C / 12F
Servings: 2 | Calories: 480
@fitmealsbykate

INGREDIENTS
300g chicken breast
1 cup cooked brown rice
2 cups baby spinach
1 tbsp olive oil
½ avocado, sliced

INSTRUCTIONS
Season the chicken and grill six minutes per side.
Slice the chicken and arrange over the rice and spinach.
Top with avocado and drizzle with olive oil.
