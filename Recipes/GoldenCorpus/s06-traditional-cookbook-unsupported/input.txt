Roast Chicken with Herbs
Ingredients
1 whole chicken
2 tbsp butter
fresh thyme
Method
Rub the chicken with butter and thyme.
Roast at 425°F until golden.

Braised Leeks
Ingredients
6 leeks
2 cups chicken stock
Method
Halve the leeks lengthwise.
Braise in stock until tender.

Apple Galette
Ingredients
3 apples
1 sheet pie dough
2 tbsp sugar
Method
Arrange the apples over the dough.
Bake until the crust is deep brown.
