Classic Buttermilk Pancakes
Fluffy diner-style pancakes ready in twenty minutes.

Ingredients
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 tbsp sugar
1 tsp baking soda
2 large eggs
2 cups buttermilk
4 tbsp unsalted butter, melted

Instructions
1. Whisk the flour, sugar, and baking soda in a large bowl.
2. Beat the eggs with the buttermilk and melted butter.
3. Fold the wet mixture into the dry until just combined.
4. Cook on a hot griddle until bubbles form, then flip.
