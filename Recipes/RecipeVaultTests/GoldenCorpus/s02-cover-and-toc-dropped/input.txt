GRILLED CHICKEN POWER BOWL
MACROS: 42P / 38C / 12F
INGREDIENTS
300g chicken breast
1 cup cooked brown rice
2 cups baby spinach
INSTRUCTIONS
Season the chicken and grill six minutes per side.
Slice and arrange over the rice and spinach.

TURKEY TACO SKILLET
MACROS: 38P / 30C / 14F
INGREDIENTS
1 lb ground turkey
1 tbsp taco seasoning
1 cup shredded cheese
INSTRUCTIONS
Brown the turkey with the seasoning.
Top with cheese and cover until melted.
