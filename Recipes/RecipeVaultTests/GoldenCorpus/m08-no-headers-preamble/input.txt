Rustic Tomato Soup
A cozy soup for rainy evenings, ready in under an hour.
2 tbsp olive oil
1 yellow onion, diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 28-oz can crushed tomatoes
2 cups vegetable broth
1. Heat the oil in a Dutch oven over medium heat.
2. Cook the onion and garlic until soft.
3. Add the tomatoes and broth and simmer 30 minutes.
4. Blend until smooth and season to taste.
