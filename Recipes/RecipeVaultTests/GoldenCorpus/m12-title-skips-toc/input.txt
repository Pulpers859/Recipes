Midnight Chocolate Chip Cookies
Makes 24 cookies

Ingredients
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup dark chocolate chips
1 cup brown sugar
2 large eggs
1 tsp vanilla extract
Instructions
1. Cream the sugar and eggs together.
2. Fold in the flour and chocolate chips.
3. Chill the dough for one hour.
4. Bake at 350°F for 11 minutes.
